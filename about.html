About Me

Prior to my life as a web developer, I studied Environmental Science and Technology Management at University of California Santa Barbara. In my last year of school I was exposed to entrepreneurship, and the power of technology. But, I found it difficult to express and implement my ideas without technical skills. In search of an outlet, I discovered coding and found myself at General Assembly; a full time, 12-week full stack web development bootcamp resulting in a work-ready skillset.

After graduating from General Assembly I decided to focus on front-end development. I am currently looking for full time work and improving my skills in Javascript, jQuery, CSS, HTML, AngularJS and UI/UX design.

Feel free to send me a message. I look forward to talking to you!
