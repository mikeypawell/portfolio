What Are The Odds

HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Javascript, Node.js Express, MongoDB, AJAX, Bootstrap, Javascript

A mobile web application of the popular game What Are The Odds. The goal of the game is to get your friends to do stupid things through dares. I focused on making this web application designed for mobile, and and intuitive enough that it can be played without explicit instructions.
